SCENE.

_A well-furnished reception-room in_ MRS. POPOV'S _home_.


LUKA.

It isn't right, ma'am. You're wearing yourself out! The maid and the cook have gone looking for berries;


MRS.POPOV.

[_Resolutely._]

don't speak of these things again. You know very well that since the death of Nikolai Michailovitch my life is absolutely nothing to me.to him how _I_ can love.
